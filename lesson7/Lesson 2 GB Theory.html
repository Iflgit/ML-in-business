Пуассон регрессия - Poission regression¶

, . .

: $$g(\eta) = \ln(\eta) \Rightarrow \hat {y} = e^{x{^T}\beta + {\beta_{0}}}.$$

, : $$\max_{\beta,\beta_0} \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^{N} \Big( y_i(x_{i}^{T}\beta + \beta_0) - e^{x{^T_i}\beta + {\beta_0}} \Big)- \phi \Big( \alpha||\beta||_1 + \dfrac {1} {2}(1 - \alpha)||\beta||^2_2 \Big),$$

  • $\phi$ , . $\phi\in\mathbb{R}^{+}$;
  • $\alpha$ , 1 ($\ell_1$) 2 ($\ell_2$). $\alpha\in[0,1]$;
  • $||\beta||{_1}$ $\ell_1$ (LASSO). $||\beta||{_1} = \sum{^p_{k=1}} |\beta{_k}|$;
  • $||\beta||{_2}$ $\ell_2$ (Ridge). $||\beta||{_2} = \sum{^p_{k=1}} \beta{^2_k}$.

Deviance : $$D = -2 \sum_{i=1}^{N} \big( y_i \text{ln}(y_i / \hat {y}_i) - (y_i - \hat {y}_i) \big).$$

Вывод метрики Deviance для GLM с распределением Пуассона¶

Deviance : "" , . $$Deviance = 2(\ell_{ideal} - \ell_{model})$$

" " . , " " GLM : $$\ell_{ideal} = \sum_{i=1}^{N}\left(y_i \ln(y_i) -y_i-\ln(y_i!)\right).$$

, , $\hat{y}_i = e^{x{^T}\beta + {\beta_{0}}}$: $$\ell_{model} = \sum_{i=1}^{N}\left(y_i \ln(\hat{y}_i) -\hat{y}_i-\ln(y_i!)\right).$$

, $$Deviance = 2\sum_{i=1}^{N}\left(y_i \ln(y_i) -y_i - y_i \ln(\hat{y}_i) +\hat{y}_i\right) = -2\sum_{i=1}^{N}\left(y_i \ln(y_i/\hat{y}_i) - (y_i -\hat{y}_i)\right).$$

, - : $$f(x;\theta,\xi) = \begin{cases} \frac{\theta^\xi}{\Gamma(\xi)}x^{\xi-1}e^{-\theta x},&x \ge 0\\ 0,& x <0 \end{cases},\hspace{20pt} \Gamma(x) = \int_0^{\infty}x^{\xi-1}e^{-x}dx. $$ , - , : $$ \mathbb{E}[x] = \frac{\xi}{\theta},\hspace{15pt} Var(x) = \frac{\xi}{\theta^2}.$$

GLM : $$ \mathbb{E}[x] = \frac{\xi}{\theta} = \mu,\hspace{15pt} Var(x) = \frac{\xi}{\theta^2} = \frac{\mu^2}{\xi}.$$ , $$f(x;\mu,\xi) = \frac{1}{\Gamma(\xi)}\theta^\xi x^{\xi-1}e^{-\theta x} = \frac{1}{\Gamma(\xi)}\left(\frac{\xi}{\mu}\right)^\xi x^{\xi-1}e^{-\left(\frac{\xi}{\mu}\right)x} = \frac{1}{x\cdot\Gamma(\xi)}\left(\frac{\xi x}{\mu}\right)^\xi e^{-\frac{\xi x}{\mu}},\hspace{15pt} x\ge 0.$$

Вывод метрики Deviance для GLM с Гамма-распределением¶

Deviance : "" , . $$Deviance = 2(\ell_{ideal} - \ell_{model})$$

" " . , " " GLM : $$\ell_{ideal} = -\sum_{i=1}^{N}\left(1 + \ln(y_i)\right).$$

, , $\hat{y}_i = e^{x{^T}\beta + {\beta_{0}}}$: $$\ell_{model} = -\sum_{i=1}^{N}\left(\frac{y_i}{\hat{y}_i} + \ln(\hat{y}_i)\right).$$

, $$Deviance = 2\sum_{i=1}^{N}\left(- 1 - \ln(y_i) + \frac{y_i}{\hat{y}_i} + \ln(\hat{y}_i)\right) = 2\sum_{i=1}^{N}\left(-\ln\left(\frac{y_i}{\hat{y}_i}\right)+\frac{y_i-\hat{y}_i}{\hat{y}_i} \right).$$
